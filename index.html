Being different shouldn’t mean being alone.

Join Saksham: Voices of Bharat — a personalized, AI-powered, community-driven platform empowering specially-abled individuals with accessible learning, mentorship, and community connection in local languages.

Key Features

login

Inclusive Login System

Easy login via email, mobile, or Google for students, parents, mentors, doctors, and volunteers with customized profiles.

dashboard

Personalized Learning Dashboard

Accessible content in audio, visual, and simplified formats with NCERT-aligned lessons and voice quizzes.

record_voice_over

Suno Meri Kahani

Share or listen to stories via text, audio, or video. Connect and interact with others facing similar challenges.

smart_toy

AI Chatbot Helper

Ask daily life questions in your language, get answers with voice & text input powered by GPT and government data.

people

Mentor Match System

Connect with verified mentors including achievers with disabilities. Schedule calls and Q&A sessions.

forum

Problem / Help Wall

Discuss personal struggles anonymously or publicly and receive community, AI, and mentor support.

From Silence to Strength

Saksham empowers millions by breaking isolation and building confidence through education, mentorship, and community support – all in local languages and accessible formats. Together, we celebrate the strength and success of specially-abled individuals across Bharat.
